My Greatest Achievement

This is a picture from my greatest achievement. During my time in the cadets, I went to many shooting competitions. There is an award that a Cadet can recieve called the crossrifle badge. You can get this award by obtaining an average score over the competition of 85%+. I did two competitions that day, one in the morning and one in the afternoon. In the morning, my average came out to 84.95% (which obviously was very disappointing). However, I managed to shoot much better in the afternoon and got an average of 89.17%, which got me my crossrifle award.

Note: Pardon the image quality, I have since left the Facebook group where it was posted and this is the only copy I have left.
