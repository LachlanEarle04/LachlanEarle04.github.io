Animation Implementation

In case it wasn't very obvious, I have in fact implemented an animation on the homepage in the background. To make it work, I gave the background a linear gradient that I found to look good and made the background itself 300% in size. For the animation, I made the keyframes move the position of the background from top left to bottom right by adjusting the position by 50% increments. I used the ease attribute to make it smoothly move back and forth between the two corners, giving the illusion of a color shifting background.
