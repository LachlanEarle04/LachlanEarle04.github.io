Pakenham, Victoria

  • - The area now called Pakenham was named after Sir Edward Pakenham, a british Major General from the 1800s
  • - Although Pakenham is quite old, it was mostly a country/farm town as similar to neighbouring towns such as Nar Nar Goon and Koo Wee Rup. It has only been recently (i.e. after 2006) that urban development has really started.
  • - The area in Pakenham I live in, Heritage Springs, is one of the fastest growing areas in pakenham with the population growing nearly 200% in the last 17 years from 19,644 in 2006 to 57,001 in 2023.
